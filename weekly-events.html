Weekly Events

Please note that event dates and times are subject to change. Please call us or check our facebook page for updates.

Mondays

6 am - 7:30 pm

Morning Yoga Class: Start your week with a relaxing yoga session.

7 pm - 9 pm

Live Acoustic Music: Enjoy soulful acoustic performances by local artists.

Tuesdays

6 am - 7:30 am

Coffee Tasting Workshop: Learn about different coffee brewing methods and taste various specialty coffees.

8 pm - 10 pm

Open Mic Night: Showcase your talent or enjoy the performances of local musicians, poets, and comedians.

Wednesdays

7 am - 7 pm

Art Exhibition: Explore the works of local artists displayed throughout the coffee shop.

7 pm - 9 pm

Trivia Night: Test your knowledge and compete with friends in a fun-filled trivia competition.

Thursdays

5 pm - 7 pm

Cooking Class: Join our skilled chef and learn to prepare delicious desserts. Limited spots available, reservations required.

6 pm - 9 pm

Board Game Night: Gather with friends and enjoy a variety of board games while sipping your favorite drinks.

Fridays

7:30 pm - 9:30 pm

Live Jazz Band: Relax and unwind with the smooth tunes of a live jazz band.

Saturdays

10 am - 12 pm (Noon)

Family Day: Bring your kids for a special day of face painting, storytelling, and fun activities.

7 pm - 9 pm

Poetry Slam: Express your creativity or listen to passionate poets share their work during our poetry slam event.

Sundays

3 pm - 4:30 pm

Book Club Meeting: Discuss the latest book selection with fellow book enthusiasts over a cup of coffee.

10 am - 2 pm

Sunday Brunch: Indulge in a delicious brunch menu accompanied by live acoustic music.
